Ryan Chidgey

Mortgage Consultant · Yellow Brick Road Home Loans
Ryan Chidgey helps first home buyers, refinancers and everyday borrowers navigate the loan process with straightforward advice, responsive service and tailored lending solutions.

Ryan focuses on making home finance feel clear, practical and easy to act on. He understands that every client has a different situation, whether they are buying their first property, reviewing their current loan, or trying to find a more suitable option for their lifestyle and financial goals.

With a client-first approach, Ryan works closely with borrowers to explain their options in plain English, keep the process moving, and help reduce the stress that often comes with property finance. His goal is to create a smooth experience from first enquiry through to submission and settlement.

I can help with

  • Advice on home loans for first home buyers
  • Refinancing to reduce repayments or improve structure
  • Guidance for self-employed and non-standard borrowers
  • Support with loan strategy, borrowing position and next steps
